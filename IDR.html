About IDR

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We emphasise the link between academic insight (UK university partnership) and real-world projects.

Training and bootcamps for students and professionals.

Advisory

Helping organisations implement frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001.
